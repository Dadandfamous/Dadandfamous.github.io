Home Section

For introducing myself to my fellow coders, I made a couple of slideshows with a little background information. Visual engagment is key here, as probably nobody will memorize a small piece of text after a long day. The images are heavily cropped and vector formatted. I found it challenging to find/create images of the same size/ratio. In addition to that, the carousel page-indicators are stretched out vertically, and are not centered.
As you can see in the third tab 'What drives me?', the vector images look ripped apart. This is probably the result of poor conversion from .jpg to .svg-files. The whole website is scalable, and shows a hamburger-navigator on smaller resolutions.

Daily Planner

I made this website initially for setting up a Daily Planner, on the Bootstrap library.
It is fully functional, and works through the menu 'Daily Planner' and 'Add Entry'.
When an event is planned, this is written to the local storage. This can be recalled in the 'Daily Planner' section, and edited or removed accordingly.
Mainly HTML5 and CSS3 are used for these pages.

Experiments

It is debatable if this section needs to be included on a page like this. However, I felt the need to test and tweak a couple of mini-projects within the Bootstrap-environment.
The code on this page is all made by myself. Meaning, I recreated a couple of beginner projects and basic coding techniques.
I am not content with the 'daymode/nightmode'-buttons. They are currently not working globally. I had issues with saving the style data to the local storage. Also, the Bootstrap 'collapse'-menus do not change to nightmode.
